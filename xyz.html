Hi there, I'm Christina! I'm a family photographer based in Woodridge, Illinois. I live for the dreamy, candid, unpredictable moments.

Donut enthusiast, plant parent, boy mama, and married to my best friend.

why should we work together?

why should we work together?

It seems like we are constantly snapping pictures of our kids with our phones, which is great. But do you know what's NOT so great? You are NOT in the pictures - and if you are, they're likely not the kinds of pictures you want to hang up on the walls.

Or maybe you're like me and you have thousands of pictures of your kids, BUT you're only in about 2% of them. Or you're one of the lucky parents who have a spouse that manages to take the WORST pictures of you imaginable.

Sounds familiar? Let's change this!

Learn More

what I do


01.


family


Do you need updated photos for your holiday cards? Have you NEVER experienced the pure joy of getting professional photos taken of your family? Are you desperately wanting to have nice photos of your kids with YOU in the photos, too?


02.


maternity


YOU mama, are growing another human. It's incredible, magical, unbelievable (and did I mention tiring?). We must document your gorgeous baby bump, whether you want to do a solo session or include your partner and kids.


03.


newborns


The littlest moments deserve the biggest celebrations. I'll be sure to capture all of the insanely adorable details of your newborn, along with sibling shots and parents with the baby.


04.


seniors


Your "baby" is about to enter adulthood and with that comes all the feelings. Be sure to capture this monumental time in their lives with a fun senior session. Senior photos are best taken the summer/fall of their senior year.

Christina is a natural light (most of the time!) photographer specializing in families, maternity, newborns and seniors. She's based in Woodridge, IL with a studio in Downers Grove and serves the surrounding DuPage County areas such as Naperville, Lisle, Wheaton, Glen Ellyn, Elmhurst, Carol Stream, Oak Brook, Warrenville, Darien, Hinsdale, etc. Available for travel as well.
