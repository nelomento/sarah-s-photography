GET IN TOUCH

Welcome to Sarah Smith Photography! I'm Sarah and I'm a portrait photographer based in London, UK. My passion for photography began at a young age, and I've been honing my skills ever since. I love to tell a story through my lens and sharing it with the world.

Portraiture

My portraiture work includes professional headshot profiles, actor headshot portfolios, and corporate shoots ranging from food & drink to PR and product photos for website and marketing imagery. I love to use color, light, and composition creatively to make people happy and have fun while doing it.

Family Photography

My family photography work includes capturing special moments of your family. I love to witness the love and connection between family and creating a fun environment. My relaxed approach to taking natural portraits puts even the most nervous people at ease!

Pet Photography

I also offer pet photography for your dog or puppy. I enjoy taking photographs of dogs of all breeds; my fun and natural portraits result in gorgeous photo prints for your wall or digital images to own and share.
